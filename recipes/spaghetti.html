Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

This is a classic spaghetti recipe that doesnt take too long to make

Ingredients

  • 8oz spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (opitional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package
    instructions until al dente. Reserve about
    1/2 cup of pasta water before draining
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over
    medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper
    flakes. Cook, stirring frequently, until the garlic
    is golden and fragrant but not browned, about 1-2 min
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the
    garlic and oil. Toss well to coat the spaghetti
    evenly. If the pasta seems dry, add a splash of
    reserved pasta water
  4. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
    Toss again to combine
  5. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and grated parmensan cheese
  6. Serve hot and enjoy this simple and flavorful pasta dish
