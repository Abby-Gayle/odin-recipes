Easy Ramen Recipe

Description

This 15min ramen meal will be a great addition to your ramen recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 package of ramen noodles cooked according to the package directions, without seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons garlic
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon everything seasoning

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter
  2. Add garlic and cook 1 minute
  3. Add soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and brown sugar. Stir to combine
  4. Push the noodles to the side and add the eggs to the empty space
  5. Scramble the eggs and mix them into noodles
  6. Season with everything seasoning. Serve warm
