Easy Roasted Broccoli Recipe

Description

This recipe will show you how to easily make roasted broccoli

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Cut broccoli florets from the stalk. Peel the stalk abnd slice inton 1/4-inch slices.
    Mix florets and steam pieces with olive oil in a bowl and transfer to a baking
    sheet season with salt and pepper
  3. Roast in oven until broccoli is tender and lightly browned, about 18 to 20 min
